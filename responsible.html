Responsible Gaming

TruthMarket is committed to promoting responsible participation. Trading on real-world outcomes involves risk and is not suitable for everyone.

18+ Only

You must be at least 18 years old to use TruthMarket.

Play Responsibly

  • Only trade what you can afford to lose
  • Take breaks and monitor your activity
  • Do not chase losses

No Guaranteed Profits

TruthMarket does not guarantee profits, outcomes, or winnings of any kind. All markets involve uncertainty.

Need Help?

If trading begins to affect your wellbeing, seek support from local responsible gaming organizations or contact us at support@truthmarket.app.
